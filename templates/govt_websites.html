🌾🌐 Government Websites for Farmers 🌐🌾

Explore trusted government portals offering valuable resources for farmers across India. From crop insurance to subsidies, these platforms provide essential information, schemes, and advisory services to support modern farming practices and financial security. Stay informed and make better agricultural decisions with these official websites.


🌱 KSDA - State Government (Karnataka) 🌱

The Raita Mitra portal by the Karnataka State Government provides agricultural schemes, subsidy details, and farming advisories tailored for Karnataka farmers. Stay updated on the latest policies and resources for sustainable agriculture.

🌍 Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (Govt. Of India) 🌍

The Central Government’s agriculture portal provides information on policies, schemes, and support programs for farmers across India. It covers initiatives related to crop production, financial aid, and technological advancements in agriculture.

💰 PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana 💰

PM-Kisan is a direct income support scheme that provides financial aid to small and marginal farmers in India. Eligible farmers receive ₹6,000 per year, helping them manage agricultural expenses efficiently.

🌾 PM Fasal Bima Yojana (Crop Insurance) 🌾

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme that protects farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities. It ensures financial stability and encourages risk-free farming.

📢 mKisan Portal 📢

The mKisan portal provides SMS-based advisory services, weather updates, and expert recommendations to farmers in their local language. It helps farmers stay informed about best practices, market prices, and weather conditions.

🧪 Department of Fertilisers (Govt. of India) 🧪

The Department of Fertilisers provides information on government subsidies, fertiliser policies, and availability. It ensures affordable and quality fertilisers reach farmers across the country.
